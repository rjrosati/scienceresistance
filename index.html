A lot of US scientists have lost their jobs to administrative attacks on federal supported science. While some of this is just a consequence of arbitrary budget cuts, most is a targeted attack on science that doesn't align with the current political and economic interests. This science is important and funding it is critical, particularly during these times.

We are a group of scientists who want to start a new funding model for science, more resilient to the whims of the administration. Sign up here if you are interested in joining a newsletter about our efforts and to be notified when we launch.
